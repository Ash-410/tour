Festivals of Karnataka

Plan your visit around vibrant cultural celebrations

Mysore Dasara

September/October

The grand state festival featuring illuminated palace, processions, and cultural events.

Hampi Utsav

November

Cultural extravaganza celebrating the glory of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Pattadakal Dance Festival

January

Classical dance performances against the backdrop of ancient temples.

Yakshagana

November/May

A traditional folk theatre form of Karnataka that combines dance, music, dialogue, costume, and storytelling to depict mythological and historical tales.
